Laura Bravo Merodio

Assistant Professor in Health Data Science & Computational Biology · University of Birmingham, UK

I am an Assistant Professor in health data science and computational biology at the University of Birmingham, based at the Centre for Health Data Science. My research studies chronic inflammation through machine learning and systems biology, with a current focus on multimorbidity, ageing and disease biology.

I work at the intersection of biomedical engineering, computational biology and applied machine learning — building pipelines that turn complex biological and clinical data into interpretable, clinically meaningful insight. I am also an educator, leading Masters modules in Health Data Science and teaching R to researchers across disciplines.
